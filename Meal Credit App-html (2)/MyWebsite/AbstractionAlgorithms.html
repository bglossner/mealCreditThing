Abstraction/Algorithm Image Gallery

Abstraction - the hiding of all but essential details for the user.

Data Abstraction - the separation of the logical view of data from its representation.

Procedural Abstraction - the separation of the logical view of an action from its implentation.


Ex: When our phone starts up, we see the screens telling us "hey, it's booting up" by showing the phone model loading screen. We don't see the thousands of lines of code being run through that do checks and startup processes required.

Algorithm - a process or set of rules to be followed in calculations or other problem-solving operations.

We see algorithms everywhere: deciding what to wear in the morning, opening an computer application, doing multiplication, fixing a bike.
Algorithms allow us to quickly, and efficiently with computers, do a set task or find solutions to a given problem.
