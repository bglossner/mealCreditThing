The Internet of Things

The Internet - a global computer network providing a variety of information and communication facilities, consisting of interconnected networks using standardized communication protocols.

    A Brief History
  • 1969 - ARPANET released
  • 1971 - Internet Protocol (IP)
  • 1974 - Telenet born (first ISP)
  • 1982 - TCP/IP emerges
  • 1983 - IPv4 Released
  • 1990 - HTML developed
  • 1991 - World Wide Web publicly released
  • 1998 - Google search engine born
  • 2005 - YouTube Launched
  • 2012 - IPv6 Released
